History of Computers

Introduction

The history of computers began with the Abacus, one of the earliest calculating devices. Later, inventors developed mechanical and electronic computers such as the Pascaline, Difference Engine, ENIAC, and modern computers. Today, computers are used in education, business, healthcare, banking, entertainment, and communication.
